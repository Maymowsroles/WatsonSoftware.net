What it does

Your clipboard, actually useful

Windows has a built-in clipboard history but it's buried, limited, and clears when you restart. CopyStack runs quietly in the system tray and keeps everything you copy in a searchable list that persists across reboots.

Press the hotkey (customizable), get a popup with your history, click the item to paste it. Pin things you use all the time — snippets, addresses, code — so they're always at the top.

  • ✓Stores unlimited clipboard history across restarts
  • ✓Supports text, images, and file references
  • ✓Searchable — type to filter the list instantly
  • ✓Pin items to keep them at the top permanently
  • ✓Customizable hotkey to open the popup
  • ✓Exclude specific apps from history (e.g. password managers)
  • ✓Tiny memory footprint — runs all day, barely noticeable
  • ✓Export and import your history
